UNIVERSITY NAME: Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent (TTPU)

ABOUT:
Turin Polytechnic University in Tashkent (TTPU) is a prestigious technical university established in 2009 in cooperation with Politecnico di Torino (Italy), UZAVTOSANOAT (Uzbekistan), and the Ministry of Higher Education of Uzbekistan. TTPU focuses on producing highly qualified engineers and technologists in line with international standards.

LANGUAGE OF INSTRUCTION:
All courses are conducted in English. Italian and Uzbek language classes are also available as electives.

TTPU offers the following scholarship programs:

- Industry Scholarships: Sponsored by local automotive and engineering companies
